How we do it?

Iron cuts Iron: We use state-of-the-art Deep Learning algorithms for DeepFake detections.
Our goal is to use AI to build innovative new technologies that can help detect deepfakes and manipulated media. Here is how we do it:

Data Prep

Data collection and preparation is the most important part of our pipeline. We train on a huge collection of publically available dataset and some proprietary sources. Given a video, we extract frames and boxes and we resize them to give the best results.

Face Extractor

We use image processing and light-weight deep learning face detector to extract faces from images. Besides a bounding box, we also extract 6 keypoints for face landmarks (2x eyes, 2x ears, nose, mouth) which helps in boosting our scores.

Training

We add some complex augmentations and processing(like, different encoding of face from other part of the image) to our input data to the model to train a classifer. We stack different models by validating the score to achieve best results.

Insightful Inference

Easy, fast, Accurate & reliable results. Available API end-points for integration. We have per-frame detection to detect any tampered/generated frames which could change the context of the video. We also have video level analytics to analyse global information of the video.
